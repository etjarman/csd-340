Chapter 1: A Little About Me

My name is Eric Jarman. I was born in Utah in January of 1994 to a family with four older brothers. Since then my parents have had two more children, making our family member total count up to nine.

I spent most of my life in Utah gorwing up in an LDS household and attending church every Sunday. This training sent me to several different military bases over the course of the next two years, while also allowing me to earn an Associate in Arts for Chinese Studies, before ending up at my duty station of Offutt AFB in Omaha, NE. By the time I separate from the Air Force, I will have served 10 years.

Since I was a child I have always enjoyed playing video games, watching movies, riding my longboard, and going to a shooting range.
